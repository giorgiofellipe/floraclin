# FloraClin Landing Page Design

## Tagline
**"Onde o cuidado floresce. Do atendimento ao financeiro."**

## Aesthetic Direction

Premium organic clinic — warm earthy tones, elegant serif headlines, soft cream backgrounds, sage/forest green accents, editorial refinement. Inspired by Dr.Sep's beauty clinic aesthetic with CloudCore's SaaS structure.

## Design Tokens

### Colors
- **Forest**: #1C2B1E (dark green — headings, primary CTAs)
- **Sage**: #4A6B52 (medium green — accents, links, section labels)
- **Mint**: #8FB49A (light green — highlights, selection)
- **Cream**: #FAF7F3 (warm white — primary background)
- **Petal**: #F2E8E1 (warm pink-beige — alternate section bg)
- **Blush**: #E8D5C8 (warm neutral — borders, inputs)
- **Sand**: #C4A882 (gold — premium accents)
- **Warm Accent**: #C2785C (terracotta — attention)
- **Charcoal**: #2A2A2A (near-black — body text)
- **Mid**: #7A7A7A (gray — secondary text)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Cormorant Garamond (serif, weight 500, tracking -0.02em)
- **Body**: DM Sans (sans-serif, weight 400)
- **Hero**: 4rem / Section: 2.5rem / Body: 1rem / Small: 0.875rem / Micro: 0.75rem
- **Section labels**: DM Sans, 0.75rem, uppercase, tracking 0.2em, sage color

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Forest bg, cream text, uppercase, 14px 32px padding, 8px radius, hover → sage
- **Secondary**: Transparent, forest text, sage border, hover → filled sage

### Cards
- White bg, 1px sage/10 border, 16px radius, 48px padding, hover lift + shadow

## Positioning

**Market Category:** Sistema de gestão para clínicas de Harmonização Orofacial
**Target Customer:** HOF clinic owners in Brazil, 1-5 practitioners, currently on WhatsApp + spreadsheets or frustrated with generic software

**Value Themes:**
1. **Feito para HOF** — face diagram, service flow, HOF-specific workflows
2. **Financeiro sem dor de cabeça** — installments, penalties, renegotiation, P&L per practitioner
3. **Prontuário digital completo** — anamnesis, photos, timeline, digital signature, face diagram

**Unique Features:**
- Freeform face diagram for injection mapping
- Outstanding UX for service flow (evaluation → approval → execution)
- Patient self-service anamnesis via tokenized link (WhatsApp)
- Multi-practitioner scheduling with overlap visualization
- Digital signature of terms and contracts
- Public clinic page for online scheduling
- Google Calendar integration + WhatsApp confirmations
- Built specifically for HOF
